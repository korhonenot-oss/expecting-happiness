About Expecting Happiness

Support. Heal. Grow.

A Safe Space for Your Journey

The path to parenthood can look different for everyone. Fertility challenges, pregnancy loss, medical appointments, uncertainty, and major life transitions can impact every part of your daily life.

My personal journey and occupational therapy background allow me to recognize the emotional, physical, and mental toll these experiences can bring. My goal is to provide compassionate support that helps you reconnect with yourself, find balance, and move forward with confidence.

Our Approach

Using an occupational therapy lens, we focus on the activities, routines, relationships, and roles that make life meaningful.

  • Supporting emotional wellness
  • Helping navigate life transitions
  • Building sustainable routines
  • Creating strategies for coping and self-care
  • Helping you feel empowered throughout your journey

Our Mission

Expecting Happiness exists to remind you that you do not have to navigate this journey alone. Every person's story deserves compassion, understanding, and support.
